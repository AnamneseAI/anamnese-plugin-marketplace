# Career Growth

Track and support the user's professional development, career goals, and skill building.

## When to Activate

- User mentions career goals or professional aspirations
- User discusses skill development or learning plans
- User talks about performance reviews or feedback
- User mentions promotions, role changes, or job transitions
- User discusses networking, mentorship, or professional relationships

## Building a Brag Document

Help the user maintain a running record of achievements:
- Save completed projects with their **impact** (not just what was done, but why it mattered)
- Use the **STAR format** for achievements: Situation, Task, Action, Result
- Record positive feedback and recognition as it happens -- don't rely on memory at review time
- Note quantifiable outcomes whenever possible (revenue impact, time saved, users affected)

## Skills Assessment and Development

When the user discusses professional growth:
- Help identify skill gaps between current role and target role
- Distinguish between **table-stakes skills** (required to keep current role) and **differentiating skills** (needed for next level)
- Suggest concrete learning actions: courses, projects, mentorship, stretch assignments
- Track progress on certifications, courses, or learning paths

## Performance Review Preparation

When reviews or feedback come up:
- Help organize achievements into themes that align with role expectations
- Structure talking points using the STAR format
- Record feedback received -- both positive and constructive
- Identify areas for improvement and turn them into actionable development goals
- Note patterns across reviews to track growth over time

## Managing Up

Help the user build visibility and alignment with leadership:
- Suggest regular status updates that highlight impact, not just activity
- Frame work in terms of business outcomes and team goals
- Prepare talking points for 1:1s with clear asks and context
- Document decisions and their rationale for future reference

## Networking and Relationships

When professional relationships come up:
- Note key contacts, mentors, and sponsors
- Suggest maintaining relationships proactively, not just when needed
- Help prepare for informational interviews or networking conversations
- Track professional communities and events relevant to the user's goals

## Career Transitions

When the user discusses job changes:
- Help clarify decision criteria (compensation, growth, culture, mission, flexibility)
- Track application progress and interview experiences
- Capture offer details and decision factors for comparison
- Prompt reflection on what the user wants from the next role, not just escape from the current one
